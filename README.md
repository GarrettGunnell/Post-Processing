* Bloom
* Color Correction
* * Exposure
* * White Balancing
* * Contrast
* * Brightness
* * Color Filtering
* * Saturation
* Tonemapping
* * RGB Clamp
* * Tumblin Rushmeier
* * Schlick
* * Ward
* * Reinhard
* * Reinhard Extended
* * Hable
* * Uchimura
* * Narkowicz ACES

* Gamma Corrector
